Origami

So I have compiled few basic origami animals. The best method of learning origami is step by step instructions.
you can find camel,chameleon,penguin,panda,cicada,and many more! Just click on the picture to get the instructions.

Camel is also known as ship of the desert
It eats thorny shrubs and last for many days without water
Its padded feet allow it to walk on sand easily
Chameleon is a reptile
Chameleon is a master in camouflaging
It can change its colour to blend in and can escape predators.
Penguins live in the antartic
They are the only known birds which are able to swim
They eat fish and use their wings for swimming
Cicadas are a family of insects.
They live in Southern Australia
Cicadas spend most of their lives underground sucking sap from tree roots.
Panda, is a bear native to south central China.
Pandas are endangered animals
They eat bamboos
Sharks are underwater predators
They can easily hunt down almost all sorts of aquatic life
They can be found in all oceans and some lakes like amazon lake.
